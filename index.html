About Dachshund Token

What is Dachshund?

Dachshund is a cryptocurrency with a unique community ownership model. 100% of the fixed token supply is locked on a decentralized exchange for purchase by anyone in the world. In the spirit of decentralized ownership and open access, no privileged entities like developers or celebrities were allocated any tokens. We believe that true community ownership is the only stable foundation of trust, and that trust is the defining characteristic of a successful currency.

Dachshund can be purchased by swapping BNB with Dachshund, and Dachshund can be traded or swapped on exchange, wallet, and marketplace. Dachshund serves as the native currency for the Dachshund ecosystem.

Our community is what drives our future. The whole project has been built by the community, and its success relies on the community, as we can only make a difference if our community is strong. As such, our project reward feature is crafted to give back to the community. We unite and take action to build a prosperous and sustainable future for us and the next generations to come.

Binance Smart Chain Product

Built on the Binance Smart Chain, Dachshund is 100x cheaper to exchange and send than Ethereum-based tokens and uses a fraction of the electricity. Dachshund leverages core blockchain innovations to deliver a global currency with Permissionless international transactions and frictionless exchange.


Binance Smart Chain is the world’s first practical blockchain, and Dachshund is the first token on BSC with the complete community ownership required to compete on a global scale with existing reserve currencies like the US dollar.

Vision

Dachshund’s Vision is a community-ran BEP-20 token on the Binance Smart Chain, created by our dev, intending to create something greater than ever in the crypto market. Our community will grow and expand across the whole world. Our goal is to offer utilities that exceed the limits of the crypto-space, reaching many niches.


The three main goals of the Dachshund Vision token are:


  ◉ Empowering the community to create a passive source of income.


  ◉ Enabling community initiatives and projects into utilities aligned with our core values.


  ◉ Rewarding holders through deflationary tokenomics and built-in burn mechanisms.

Reward

As per the contract, Dachshund holders automatically receive a distribution of 2% of each transaction of the Dachshund token. This function is a unique and easy way for holders to receive rewards directly to their wallets without the need to stake or farm on other platforms. This saves gas fees and allows compounding with ease of mind.


The passive staking rewards from holding Dachshund increase rapidly as Dachshund gains more recognition and thus more transaction volume. "To see growing numbers of Dachshund in your wallet just by holding is priceless."


Charity

It is our understanding that ultimately, all our actions must contribute to a better world, where interaction between people is made easy and friendly. Dachshund wants to help people, the environment, and animal who is in danger and who need our help. 1% of the transaction tax will be funded to charities around the world.


Our global environment must be preserved and protected by all means. Dachshund Vision aims to develop utilities that will encourage any positive endeavor toward our planet and help to create a sustainable world for future generations.

FAQs

PANCAKESWAP STEP-BY-STEP?

Download the metamask?

Download the metamask extension and create a wallet. Then proceed to fund your wallet with BNB. Make sure you have enough for gas fees.

Connect your wallet?

Access Pancakeswap and connect your wallet to the DAPP. Make sure you are connecting to the correct url as Pancakeswap.finance.

Import the Dachshund contract?

You will need to add the contract to your wallet to swap Dachshund. Simply click “add token” to your wallet and paste the Minionsinu contract, then click “import”..

Swap BNB to Dachshund?

After importing, click “select Dachshund token” and then enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap. Click “swap” and you will receive LARD tokens in your wallet.
