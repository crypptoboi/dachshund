What is Dachshund?

FAQs

PANCAKESWAP STEP-BY-STEP?

Download the metamask?

Download the metamask extension and create a wallet. Then proceed to fund your wallet with BNB. Make sure you have enough for gas fees.

Connect your wallet?

Access Pancakeswap and connect your wallet to the DAPP. Make sure you are connecting to the correct url as Pancakeswap.finance.

Import the Dachshund contract?

You will need to add the contract to your wallet to swap Dachshund. Simply click “add token” to your wallet and paste the Minionsinu contract, then click “import”..

Swap BNB to Dachshund?

After importing, click “select Dachshund token” and then enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap. Click “swap” and you will receive LARD tokens in your wallet.
